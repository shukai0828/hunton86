Short Headlines, Slide To Top Accordion

This slide to top accordion is for short headers, due to the fact that it has a fixed height header, which is necessary for it to work correctly. If you have a long page of just accordion items, and they happen to be short headers, then this is much better than the average accordion. If, however, you have a page of toggle items with long titles, you'd want to use something else.
